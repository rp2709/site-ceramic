Shaping Earth Into Art

Terraformart is a community-driven ceramic exposition born from the collaboration of many artists in and around Lausanne. Instead of following a single theme, it celebrates the richness that comes when different visions come together in one place. You will find pieces that are delicate and refined next to works that are bold, experimental, and playful. Each artist brings their own story, techniques, and inspirations, creating a lively mix that reflects the diversity of our ceramic community. More than just an exhibition, Terraformart is an open invitation to discover, to be surprised, and to enjoy the many ways clay can be shaped into art.
